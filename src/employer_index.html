How to Post a Job

1.

Create a Listing

Start by clicking the "Post New Job" button. Simply fill in the job details, including the role, requirements, and application deadline. Our streamlined form ensures a smooth process, so your listing goes live quickly.

2.

Get Approved

Our team quickly reviews your job posting to ensure clarity and relevance. Once approved, your listing goes live, making it visible to students searching for opportunities.

3.

Connect & Hire

Browse applications, filter candidates, and connect with the best fit. Reach out to schedule interviews and finalize hiring—all within a seamless process designed for efficiency.
